Who is it for?

Who is it for?

Our Keto score is for anyone on a ketogenic diet who wants a clear answer to the question Is it keto? It also helps people struggling to understand the role of protein in ketogenic diets - which admittedly can be complicated.

Our Keto score and its simple labels provide answers to all of these questions, making it easier than ever to follow your well-formulated ketogenic diet.

How does it work?

How does it work?

Our Keto score is the first of its kind, developed by Nutrita’s team of scientists and engineers who took a deep dive into metabolism and nutrition. The score is derived from clinical studies measuring hormonal changes after people have eaten certain foods.

The best way to use the Keto score is to use it alongside our Nutrient density score. Why is that? Using both scores allows you to follow a well-formulated ketogenic diet, which is by definition nutrient dense and ketogenic. You get the best of both worlds.
