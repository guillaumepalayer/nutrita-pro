What we believe in, what we stand for, and why we’re building Nutrita

What we believe in, what we stand for, and why we’re building Nutrita

We want to get the science right and do good by our audience and users.

To do that, we write about topics that interest our audience and that our team have studied deeply. Accurate, actionable and interesting is what we aim for.

Our content team includes Ph.D. research scientists, copy editors, graphic designers and engineers, all lending their professional expertise to produce great content. We’re careful to reference key claims with numbers [1] linking to a particular scientific study for anyone to verify independently.

When producing content, our team judiciously considers clinical evidence, animal models, observational data, computational models, clinical case-reports and considerations stemming evolutionary biology.
