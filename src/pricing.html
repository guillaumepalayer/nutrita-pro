Frequently asked questions

Frequently asked questions

How does the 30-day trial work?

Cancel at any time in your account settings. You won't be charged if you cancel before 28 Apr 2019.

How do I pay for your service?

At the end of your free trial, you will be charged automatically in order to continue to use the service. You can choose between Monthly and Yearly billing. We accept credit & debit cards, PayPal, Google Pay, and Apple Pay.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your plan anytime. When your subscription period expires, you will lose access to your Nutrita account.

I have a different question…

Explore our Help Center, or just ask via e-mail at hello@nutrita.app

Can I recover an expired account?

You can recover an expired account by buying a plan and re-access everything you've been tracking. Your data will remain intact for 14 days.
